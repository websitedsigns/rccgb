Great Yarmouth Trip Report – June 2025

Our summer seaside trip took us to Great Yarmouth, enjoying Joyland, the classic wooden Roller Coaster, and seafront attractions.

Summer Bash 2025
Great Yarmouth Pleasure Beach – Saturday 28th June
125 members attended (our first visit since 2008!)
Exclusive Ride Session & Joyland Add-On

Members enjoyed ERS, behind-the-scenes tours, and later visited Joyland rides including Super Snails, Tyrolean Tubs, Jet Cars, and Neptune’s Kingdom.

Final Thoughts

Fantastic day visiting both parks. Warmly welcomed by staff and engineers. Thanks to all attendees, including 18 first-timers!
