Great Yarmouth Trip Report – June 2025

Our summer seaside trip took us to Great Yarmouth, where members enjoyed Joyland, the classic wooden Roller Coaster at the Pleasure Beach, and a full day of fun at the seafront attractions.

Summer Bash 2025
Great Yarmouth Pleasure Beach – Saturday 28th June
125 members attended (our first visit since 2008!)
Exclusive Ride Session (ERS)

We met at the Pleasure Beach at 9:30am. Wristbands issued, bonus crazy golf token! Then the legendary 1932 wooden Roller Coaster.

Small groups got behind-the-scenes tour with engineers explaining backstage operations.

Freetime in the Park

After ERS, members enjoyed 3 other coasters and thrill rides. Special guests:

  • "Ori", with trip badge – all day (not coaster!)
  • John Musk, celebrating 78th birthday, founding member.
Joyland Add-On

After Pleasure Beach closed, 102 members visited Joyland, just a short walk along the prom. Nostalgic rides included:

  • Super Snails (since 1949)
  • Tyrolean Tubs – last operating Virginia Reel ride in the world
  • Jet Cars, Neptune’s Kingdom, and a small coaster
Final Thoughts

Fantastic day visiting both parks. Warmly welcomed by staff and engineers. Sun even came out! Thanks to all attendees, including 18 first-timers!
